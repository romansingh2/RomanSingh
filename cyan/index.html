About Me

I am a physics graduate from McMaster University, with a Bachelor's in chemical and physical sciences. Originally wanting to go into research, the further I went into my program the more I realized I preferred application as opposed to studying theory. In other words, I want to build extraordinary products for the world, instead of writing greek letters on a whiteboard. Since graduating, I leveraged the problem solving and analytical skills I learned from physics to build useful web apps, both frontend and backend. What drives me most is my desire to learn, push myself, and constantly improve.

Outside of coding, I love to exercise! I believe your body should be as strong as your mind, because if you do not have health than you have nothing. My favourite forms of exercise are weight training, dance, and running with my dog. Since the Covid-19 pandemic began I turned my garage into a home gym/workout studio. Pushing myself physically has given me the endurance to push myself mentally as well (I don't know what's worse, heavy squats or debugging code).
